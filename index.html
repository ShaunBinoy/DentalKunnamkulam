Meet our Chief

Dr. Eldho J Varghese

Dr Eldho J Varghese completed his B.D.S from M R Ambedhkar Dental College and Hospital, Banglore in 2017 and completed his M.D.S from prestigious SRM University, SRM Kattankulathur Dental College and Hospital Tamil Nadu in 2021. He is currently working as assistant professor in Royal Dental College, Palakkad (2021- till date). He is currently working as consultant endodontist and restorative dentist in various clinics in and outside Thrissur. He has various publications internationally published and has a patent filed in his name as one of the inventors. He is a strong believer of Jesus Christ and is readily available to serve the needy.
